Bio

Front-end and back-end software developer since 2018. I went to Itawamba Community College (2018- 2019) and Mississippi State University (2020- 2022) where I pursued a degree in Computer Information Systems. After graduating from my degree program, I have done independent development through game design within game engines such as Unity using C# and Unreal Engine 5 using their Blueprints coding framework. I recently was accepted by Mississippi Coding Academies, a full-stack development work study, where I have conducted a multitude of projects that include back-end and front-end developer skillsets. I hope to pursue a field in full-stack or back-end development when I graduate from the MCA program. In my free time, I love learning new things, content creation, teaching others about software development, and appreciating game design in video games.
